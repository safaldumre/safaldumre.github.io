SnackShop
I founded SnackShop as a solution to the problem that I and many students encounter on campus. The problem was that late-night snacking was time-consuming, expensive and an all-around difficult process. SnackShop solves this problem by providing 5$ boxes of snacks that are delivered right to your dorm room door in under 10 minutes. SnackShop offers students cheaper snacks of the same quality with less effort required.
Dissuo
Dissuo is a medical device that allows patients to remove stiches at home. It eliminates the need to return to the doctor to remove stiches saving users time and money. This project came to fruition through a whitespace competition in which our team advanced to the national stage. We are planning to patent this device as well.
